Chart Examples & Usage

Pie Chart

Best for showing parts of a whole. Use with categorical data where you want to show proportions.

Example: Market share, budget allocation, survey responses

Bar Chart

Best for comparing different categories. Shows discrete data clearly.

Example: Sales by region, population by age group

Histogram

Shows distribution of continuous numerical data by grouping into bins.

Example: Height distribution, test scores, income ranges

Frequency Polygon

Line chart connecting midpoints of histogram bins. Good for comparing distributions.

Example: Comparing test score distributions across classes

Frequency Curve

Smooth curve showing frequency distribution. Helps visualize data patterns.

Example: Normal distribution visualization, density curves

Ogive Curve

Shows cumulative frequency. Useful for finding percentiles and quartiles.

Example: Finding median, quartiles, percentile ranks
